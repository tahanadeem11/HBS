Occupied Home Staging

Stage your home while you're still living in it. Our occupied home staging service works with your existing furniture and belongings to create a market-ready presentation. We declutter, reorganize, and enhance your space to maximize appeal to potential buyers while maintaining your comfort and lifestyle during the selling process.

What is occupied home staging?

Occupied home staging involves working with your existing furniture and belongings to create a market-ready presentation. We declutter, reorganize, and enhance your space to maximize buyer appeal while you continue living in the home during the selling process.

Do I need to move out during staging?

No, you can continue living in your home during occupied staging. We work around your schedule and make strategic changes that enhance your home's appeal while maintaining your comfort and daily routine.

What happens to my personal items?

We help you depersonalize your space by storing personal photos, memorabilia, and excess items. This helps buyers visualize themselves in the home while we strategically display neutral decor that appeals to a wide range of buyers.
