What is partial home staging?

Partial home staging focuses on staging key rooms that have the most impact on buyers, such as the living room, master bedroom, and kitchen. This cost-effective approach maximizes your staging investment by creating strong first impressions in the most important spaces.

Which rooms should be staged?

We typically stage the living room, dining room, master bedroom, and kitchen as these are the rooms buyers focus on most. However, we can customize the staging plan based on your home's unique features and your budget.

How much does partial staging cost?

Partial staging costs significantly less than full home staging while still providing professional results. Pricing depends on the number of rooms staged and the furniture/decor needed. Contact us for a customized quote based on your specific needs.
